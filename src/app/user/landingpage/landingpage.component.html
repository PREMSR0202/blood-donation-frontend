Donate blood, save life

Blood way is an initiative started in the year 2022 to connect people searching for blood and donors .Blood donation is an extremely noble deed, yet there is a scarcity of regular donors across India. We focus on creating & expanding a virtual army of blood donating volunteers who could be searched and contacted by family / care givers of a patient in times of need.




Types of Blood Donation


Whole Blood

Who can donate : You need to be 18-65 years old, weigh 45kg or more and be fit and healthy.

Used for : Stomach disease, kidney disease, childbirth, operations, blood loss, trauma, cancer, blood diseases, haemophilia, anemia, heart disease.

Plasma

Who can donate : You need to be 18-70 (men) or 20-70 (women) years old, weigh 50kg or more and must have given successful whole blood donation in last two years.

Used for :Immune system conditions, pregnancy, bleeding and nerve conditions, haemophilia, immunisations.

Platelets

Who can donate :You need to be 18-70 years old (men), weigh 50kg or more and have given a successful plasma donation in the past 12 months

Used for : Cancer, blood diseases, haemophilia, anaemia, childbirth, trauma, burns.
